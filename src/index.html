Ticket Command Details

The `/ticket` command is designed to streamline your support operations. Users can create new tickets for various issues, and staff can efficiently manage and resolve them.

  • Create new support tickets easily.
  • Assign tickets to specific staff members.
  • Add notes and updates to ticket conversations.
  • Close or re-open tickets as needed.
  • Integrates with custom channels for ticket logging.

Invite Command Details
